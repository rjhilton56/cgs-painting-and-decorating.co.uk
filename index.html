What We Do

Our Services

From a single feature wall to a complete home makeover — no job is too big or too small.

Interior Painting

Complete interior painting including ceilings, walls, woodwork, and trims. Flawless finish every time.

Hallways, Stairs & Landings

Specialist work on often-tricky hallway and stairwell areas — painted to perfection.

Wallpapering & Feature Walls

Expert wallpaper hanging and stunning feature walls to transform any room.

Full Room Makeovers

Bedrooms, living rooms, kitchens — full decorating service from preparation to final coat.

Commercial Work

Offices, rental properties, commercial spaces — professional results with minimal disruption.

Burn-Off Paint Removal

Using a hot air gun to strip old paint from door surrounds, architraves, and woodwork — faster than sanding and leaves a far cleaner, smoother surface for the final finish.

Wallpaper Stripping

Full wallpaper removal available ahead of plastering or painting. Walls properly prepped and ready for the next stage.

External Painting

External walls, fencing, and garden structures painted to protect and refresh. Available on request — ask when booking your quote.
